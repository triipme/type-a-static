DAY 01

Asia’s Happiest Country

The flight in is spectacular, including views of Mt. Everest, Kanchenjunga, and Makula. Welcome to Bhutan.

DAY 02

The Views Outside, and In

We will explore Thimphu and have a group sharing and meditation with a high ranking lama.

DAY 03

Bridging the Gap

This day we will visit many beautiful places, such as Punakha (1300m) with its 160 meter suspension bridge, and also eat at a traditional farmhouse.

DAY 04

Live Like a Local

Experience life like a local Bhutanese, including a visit to a local farmhouse with homemade food and gorgeous nature.

DAY 05

Into the Mountain

Tiger’s Nest is a prominent Himalayan Buddhist sacred site and the temple complex is located in the cliffside of the upper Paro valley in Bhutan.

DAY 06

Share. Explore. Connect.

We will have a sharing session with Bhutanese business leaders and students to learn about business and entrepreneurship in Bhutan.

DAY 07

The Journey Begins

Our final Type-A Breakfast and sharing before catching the flight home. Let the journey begin.
